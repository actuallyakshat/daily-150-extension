Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4th March 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using the Daily-150 Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It only accesses your browsing activity to check if you have completed a journal entry before allowing access to social media websites.

3. Permissions

The extension requires access to Chrome storage to save your journaling status and block social media websites until an entry is made.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share data with third parties. However, if you log into our journaling platform (Daily-150), that website may have its own privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be communicated through the extension or our website.
